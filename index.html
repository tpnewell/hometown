Palmer Lake

Enjoy a cozy atmosphere in Colorado's small town

Top three activities to do at Palmer Lake

Hike

There are several scenic hikes around the town, including the Palmer Lake Resevoir Trail

Grill

The local pub is the greatest-- be sure to stop by O'Malley's Steak Pub to grill your own steak

See Town Star

If you find yourself in town during late November - December, you'll see the Palmer Lake Star light up the hillside at nighy
